Todays Topic: Cell

Definition of Cell

Cell is the structural and functional unit of life, all living things are made up of one or more cells,
organism that posses one cell are called unicellular organism, while those with more than one cell are
grouped under multicellular organism

Types of Cell

There are two major types of cell, these are;
  1. Plant cell
  2. Animal cell
